# Tier A / B artist prioritization

Use **wiki depth** plus **Google Search Console** to decide where to invest internal links, hero drops, and meta experiments.

## Rubric

| Signal | Tier A (invest) | Tier B (maintain) |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Wiki entity | Rich **SEO** block (title, meta, keywords), **FAQ**, **press** or exhibitions, **portfolio images** | Thinner bio; fewer proof assets |
| GSC | URLs with impressions or strategic commercial intent | Long tail / new |
| Business | Drops, PR, partnership committed | Catalogue fill |

## How to use wiki entities

1. Open `wiki/entities/<slug>.md` for each candidate.
2. Confirm sections: **Overview**, **SEO** (target keywords), **FAQ**, **Press** / **Exhibitions**, **Portfolio Images** where present.
3. Cross-check live URL: `https://www.thestreetcollector.com/shop/artists/<slug>`
4. Pull GSC performance for that URL — high impressions + low CTR → title/description test using keywords from the wiki **SEO** block.

## Starter note

Most ingested artists include a **`## SEO`** block from research passes. Use **Tier A** = intersection of **strong wiki** + **GSC opportunity** + **business priority** (do not tier on wiki alone).
