# Positioning wedge (single source for site, PR, artist briefs)

Use **the same story everywhere**: packaging, PDP, artist outreach, press, affiliate copy.

## The three legs

1. **Physical object** — Premium **illuminated display** built for **swap-in limited-edition prints** (not generic poster lights or commodity frames).
2. **Art layer** — **Independent / street-art-adjacent** artists, **small runs**, **edition scarcity**, **Certificate of Authenticity** (COA).
3. **Emotional job** — A **living collection**: display, rotate, collect — bridges **home / lifestyle** and **contemporary art buying**.

## Lines we repeat

- Limited editions; finite runs; COA.
- Worldwide shipping where true; edition numbering where applicable.
- **Street Collector** as the **platform + lamp + authenticity** story — avoid sounding like “only a print shop.”

## What we avoid diluting into (unless intentional capture pages)

- Generic “wall art” / mass posters.
- Commodity LED frames without the **edition + artist + COA** narrative.
