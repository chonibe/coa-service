# Paola Delfin

Paola Delfín is a Mexican muralist whose large-scale women seem to grow from the wall itself.

## Overview

Paola Delfín is a Mexican muralist whose large-scale women seem to grow from the wall itself. Black, white, and soft gray carry the emotion without raising their voice — curves that echo Art Nouveau and street timing in the same breath. Her figures have appeared in cities across the Americas and Europe, and each one feels like it belongs to the neighborhood it lives in.
