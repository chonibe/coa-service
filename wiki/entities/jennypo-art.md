# Jennypo Art

Jenny Pokryvailo is a tattooist and illustrator whose line never forgets skin.

## Overview

Jenny Pokryvailo is a tattooist and illustrator whose line never forgets skin. Whether the surface is body or paper, the drawing stays decisive — ornament, narrative, and negative space in careful balance. Fans follow for the same reason clients book: every mark earns its place.
