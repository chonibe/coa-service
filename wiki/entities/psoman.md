# Psoman

Psoman studied graphic design in Belgium and welding and polyester fabrication in Switzerland before committing to painting full time.

## Overview

Psoman studied graphic design in Belgium and welding and polyester fabrication in Switzerland before committing to painting full time. Hybrid animals and surreal bodies carry quiet warnings about nature under pressure across walls in Europe and Asia. A school residency in Taiwan in 2024 proved the characters travel without losing anything. The STRAAT Museum in Amsterdam holds one of his pieces.

## Additional History & CV

*Research notes — verify facts against primary sources before publishing.*

This Belgian humble artist knows how to makes us look a little longer to his paintings on walls or on canvas… just to absorb all the details he leaves behind. I studied Graphic design but naturally evolved to drawing and painting.
Web research (verify): Psoman / Pso Man—born 1981, Brussels area (Liège region per tour writeups). Graphic design studies Belgium 2001; further 3D/welding/polyester training Switzerland. Muralist, painter, sculptor, illustrator; themes of nature, climate, urbanization; work in STRAAT Museum Amsterdam collection and Artsy listing. Active in festivals across Europe and Asia since 2001 (self-reported—confirm on psoman.co).
