# Thibaud Herem

Thibaud Hérem is a French illustrator known for hand-drawn architectural work in pencil and Indian ink.

## Overview

Thibaud Hérem is a French illustrator known for hand-drawn architectural work in pencil and Indian ink. The buildings feel walked, not photographed — facades rendered with an obsessive patience that turns a structure into a portrait. He has worked for Hermès, Netflix (Our Beloved Summer), and Four Seasons, published books with Nobrow and Laurence King, and held a solo show in Seoul.
