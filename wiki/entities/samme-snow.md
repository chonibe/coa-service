# Samme Snow

Samme Snow finished a Graphic Communication degree in Farnham in 2013 and chose walls over office work.

## Overview

Samme Snow finished a Graphic Communication degree in Farnham in 2013 and chose walls over office work. He has painted corporate lobbies, skate spaces, and magazine editorial spreads with the same generous, unhurried line. Since 2020 he has run open coloring workshops with thousands of participants, which feels continuous with the mural practice rather than separate from it.
