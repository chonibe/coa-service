# Wuper Kec

Wuper Kec is a Serbian muralist who has been doing graffiti since 2006 and large-scale brush work for over five years.

## Overview

Wuper Kec is a Serbian muralist who has been doing graffiti since 2006 and large-scale brush work for over five years. Wolves, birds, and human hybrids hold walls across Europe. Fairy tale on first look, something stranger on the second.

## Key Facts

- **Location**: Indjija, Serbia
- **Active since**: Graffiti since 2006; brush murals for ~5+ years per Boulogne-sur-Mer blog (2024)
- **Instagram**: @wuper_kec
- **Portfolio / About**: https://streetart.boulogne-sur-mer.fr/wuper-kec/
