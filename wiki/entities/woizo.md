# Woizo

Woizo is a painter and muralist based in Toulouse who fills walls with narrative — creatures standing in for human impulses, better and worse.

## Overview

Woizo is a painter and muralist based in Toulouse who fills walls with narrative — creatures standing in for human impulses, better and worse. Graphic clarity keeps the story readable from across the street; detail rewards getting closer. Humor, unease, and genuine beauty in the same frame.
