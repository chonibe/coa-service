## Overview

Craig Watkins is Wotto — a British illustrator based in Orange County who has been freelancing since graduating with a BA in Illustration and Design in 2000. The practice has covered toys, apparel, advertising, and personal character universes in the years since. UK wit, California color, pop surrealism for people who read the label.
