# kymo_one

Kymo One is a Belgian muralist trained in graphic illustration who never gave up spray paint. (kymo_one)

## Overview

Kymo One is a Belgian muralist trained in graphic illustration who never gave up spray paint. Chains, roses, smoke, and industrial romanticism repeat across European walls — old-master drama at graffiti velocity. Readable from the sidewalk and worth getting closer to.

## Key Facts

- **Location**: Ghent, Belgium (Behance, Dutch Wikipedia, kymoone.be)
- **Active since**: Active in documented murals from at least 2018 onward per Dutch Wikipedia project list
- **Instagram**: kymo_one
