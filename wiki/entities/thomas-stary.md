# Thomas Stary

Tomáš Starý is a Prague designer who trained in industrial design at the University of West Bohemia and has been painting murals since 2010.

## Overview

Tomáš Starý is a Prague designer who trained in industrial design at the University of West Bohemia and has been painting murals since 2010. His walls treat the city like a typographic poster — geometry, rhythm, and the kind of balance that industrial design training quietly enforces. The same logic runs through illustration work and architectural-scale pieces alike.
