# Dawal

Dawal started painting the streets of Paris around 18.

## Overview

Dawal started painting the streets of Paris around 18. His thing is working with what the city already gives him — peeling plaster, cracked walls, odd shapes in aging surfaces — and placing miniature surreal scenes inside them. Images that stop you mid-pavement. He runs an open studio in the 18th arrondissement and has had solo shows at Galerie Artistik Rezo, where the same instinct moves from wall to canvas without losing its intimacy.

## Additional History & CV

*Research notes — verify facts against primary sources before publishing.*

Web research (verify): Self-taught French street artist based in Paris; born 1994. Began painting in streets around 18–19, influenced by hip-hop, moving from lettering toward character-driven work (Posca interview). Open studio at Shakirail, Paris 18e, since Oct 2023. Practice includes murals, canvas, drawing, sculpture; transforms wall imperfections into miniature dreamlike scenes (Creapills and other press).
