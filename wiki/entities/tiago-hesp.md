# Tiago Hesp

Tiago Hesp was born in 1981, trained in scenography at Escola Superior de Teatro e Cinema in Lisbon, and has been doing graffiti since 1998. (tiagohesp)

## Overview

Tiago Hesp was born in 1981, trained in scenography at Escola Superior de Teatro e Cinema in Lisbon, and has been doing graffiti since 1998. His practice now spans mural painting, music, illustration, and theatre design — disciplines that share a grammar of stage and surface. He describes painting as starting from an aggressive black line and chasing balance between shadow and color.

BIOGRAPHY Tiago Hesp (1981), is a Portuguese artist with a long multidisciplinary artistic career that takes expressions from mural art, to music, illustration, scenography and painting. Graduated in Scenography from the Escola Superior de Teatro e Cinema de Lisboa (formerly the Lisbon Conservatory), Tiago Hesp has been exploring multiple techniques and approaches to his art, having gained considerable visibility for his work in graffiti, which he has been developing since 1998.
