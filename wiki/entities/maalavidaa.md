# Maalavidaa

Alycia Rainaud built Maalavidaa around a 2018 thesis on the role of psychology in design.

## Overview

Alycia Rainaud built Maalavidaa around a 2018 thesis on the role of psychology in design. The neon gradients and saturated abstraction aren't decorative — they're the argument: color as a tool for emotional regulation, rhythm as something you can actually feel. The work doesn't tell you how to feel. It gives you something to feel with.

## Additional History & CV

*Research notes — verify facts against primary sources before publishing.*

Maalavidaa | About is Alycia Rainaud , a French Graphic Designer and Digital Artist based in Montréal, Canada. She created Maalavidaa in 2016 as both a persona and an artistic mindset, while graduating in June 2018 with a Master Degree in Graphic Design and a thesis dealing with the importance of Psychology in Design. With this aim as a work thread, she founded Heal The Deal, Web3’s first emotional support club, a safe space driven by creativity and empathy, as well as a gateway to a new path in Alycia’s visual journey.
Web research (verify): Maalavidaa is the persona of Alycia Rainaud, French graphic designer and digital artist based in Montréal, QC, Canada; brand/persona founded 2016. Education: Master’s graphic design DSAA Lycée Denis Diderot Marseille (2018, thesis on psychology in design); prior graphic design training ESDAC Aix-en-Provence. Practice ties abstract neon aesthetics to Jungian therapy, color therapy, meditation; founder of Heal The Deal (2021) and Creative Director at 0x Society (verify titles/dates on site).
