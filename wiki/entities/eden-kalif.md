# Eden Kalif

Eden Kalif is an illustrator and tattoo artist who graduated Bezalel and kept both practices going in parallel. (_e_d_e_n_)

## Overview

Eden Kalif is an illustrator and tattoo artist who graduated Bezalel and kept both practices going in parallel. His line does the heavy lifting — faces, hands, and symbols with almost nothing extra. Clean, precise, and consistent whether the surface is paper, skin, or a print run.

## Key Facts

- **Location**: Tel Aviv, Israel (multiple print shops and Artinrug)
- **Active since**: Post-Bezalel professional practice (Artinrug, GoodMood artist pages)
- **Instagram**: _e_d_e_n_
- **Portfolio / About**: https://artinrug.com/artist/eden-kalif/
- **Shop collection**: https://thestreetcollector.com/collections/eden-kalif
