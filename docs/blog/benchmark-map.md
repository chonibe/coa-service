## Recurring topic clusters
- Buying guides for originals, editions, and prints
- Authentication, provenance, and legitimacy
- Artist watchlists and scene radar
- City guides and neighborhood mapping
- Festival, fair, and exhibition momentum
- Public-wall practice versus collectible-format translation
- Materials, process, and display
- Entry-level collector pathways

## Street Collector response angles
- Translate scene noise into collector clarity
- Own the “wall to room” conversation
- Stay practical about editions, condition, and format fit
- Be more honest than pure sales platforms about hype and taste risk
- Use recurring franchises instead of isolated posts
