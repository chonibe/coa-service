**Onboarding:**
- `GET /api/vendor/onboarding/status` - Check onboarding progress
- `POST /api/vendor/onboarding/complete` - Mark onboarding complete

**Perks & Benefits:**
- `POST /api/vendor/products/[id]/benefits` - Add benefits to product
- `GET /api/collector/products/[id]/benefits` - View unlocked benefits
- `POST /api/collector/benefits/[id]/claim` - Claim benefit

**Credits:**
- `GET /api/banking/balance` - Get credit + cash balance
- `POST /api/banking/credits/spend` - Spend credits on purchase
- `GET /api/banking/credits/history` - Transaction history

**Locked Pricing:**
- `GET /api/collector/products/[id]/price-visibility` - Check if price is visible
- `POST /api/vendor/products/[id]/lock-price` - Configure price lock
