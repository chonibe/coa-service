# First Edition Reserve - Legal Compliance Documentation

## Overview

This document outlines the legal compliance measures implemented for the First Edition Reserve System to ensure transparency and proper disclosure to vendors.

## Legal Requirements Met

### 1. Vendor Terms of Service

**File**: `docs/features/vendor-terms/VENDOR_TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`

Created comprehensive vendor terms that include:
- Clear explanation of First Edition Reserve Policy
- Commission structure (25% payout)
- Disclosure that edition #1 is reserved
- Statement that public sales start from edition #2
- Clarification that IP rights are not affected
- Benefits of being in the collection

### 2. Onboarding Terms Acceptance

**File**: `app/vendor/components/onboarding-wizard.tsx`

Added terms acceptance step that:
- Shows before profile completion
- Requires checkbox acceptance of Vendor Terms of Service
- Includes summary of First Edition Reserve Policy
- Links to full terms document
- Prevents completion without acceptance

### 3. Vendor Settings Access

**File**: `app/vendor/dashboard/settings/page.tsx`

Added:
- Link to Vendor Terms of Service
- Summary of First Edition Reserve Policy
- Easy access for vendors to review terms

## Legal Framing

### Transaction Type
The reserve is framed as:
- **"First Edition Reservation with Artist Commission"**
- Not a full purchase, but a reservation
- Artist receives standard 25% commission
- Platform reserves the first edition

### Payment Structure
- Order total reflects only the payout amount (25%)
- Line item shows full product price for reference
- Clear separation between product value and actual payment

### Documentation
- Vendor Terms clearly state the policy
- Disclosure shown at multiple points
- Terms accessible at all times
- No hidden or surprise charges

1. **Onboarding**: Vendor signs up and accepts Vendor Terms of Service (including First Edition Reserve Policy)
2. **Product Submission**: Vendor submits products (terms already accepted)
3. **Approval**: First edition automatically reserved per accepted terms
4. **Settings**: Terms always accessible for review

## Key Legal Points

### What Vendors Agree To
- First edition (#1) is reserved for Street Collector collection
- They receive 25% commission on the reserved edition
- Public sales start from edition #2
- This does not affect their IP rights
- This does not prevent additional sales

### What Vendors Keep
- Intellectual property rights
- Ability to sell editions #2+
- Standard 25% commission on all sales
- Full control over their artwork

### What Platform Gets
- Right to reserve edition #1
- First edition in collection
- Pays only 25% (artist commission)
- Maintains collection for brand value

## Industry Precedent

Similar practices exist in:
- Art galleries (maintain permanent collections)
- Publishing (publisher's copies)
- Record labels (label copies)
- All properly disclosed in contracts

The First Edition Reserve System is legally compliant through:
